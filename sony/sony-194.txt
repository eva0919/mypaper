<title:>BUZZ- Sony  Corp: U.S.-listed stock headed for worst week in 10 months
<Date:>18 September 2014
<Content:>
** The Japanese consumer electronics maker's U.S.-listed shares on track for their worst week since November 2013
** After a 6.8 pct fall on Wednesday, stock falls as much as 5 pct to $17.93 on Thursday
** Shares had risen by a third from their 52-week low of $15.24 on Feb. 3 through Tuesday's close of $20.25
** Company on Wednesday cut its profit outlook for the sixth time on CEO Kazuo Hirai's watch
** Sony's Tokyo-listed shares fell more than 10 pct on Thursday in their biggest drop in more than 10 months
