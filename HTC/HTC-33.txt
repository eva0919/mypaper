<title:>HTC stock jumps on market-beating Q3 profit
<Date:>5 October 2014
<Content:>
TAIPEI, Oct 6 (Reuters) - Shares of smartphone maker HTC rose almost 2 percent on Monday as investor sentiment was buoyed by its latest quarterly profit that topped market expectations.
At the opening bell, HTC was up 1.9 percent, outpacing the main index's 0.13 percent gain. (Reporting by Faith Hung; Editing by Prateek Chatterjee)
