<title:>Taiwan's HTC says sees year-on-year revenue growth in Q4
<Date:>31 July 2014
<Content:>
TAIPEI, July 31 (Reuters) - Taiwan's HTC said the company will see overall revenue growth in the October to December period, its first quarter of year-on-year growth since the third quarter of 2011.
The company's chief financial officer gave the prediction after the company said that third-quarter revenue this year is likely to decline slightly from the same period of 2013. (Reporting by Michael Gold)
